Ingredients Quantity Instructions
Flour 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour Mix with other dry ingredients.
Baking Powder 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder Mix with flour.
Sugar 1 tablespoon white sugar Add to the dry ingredients.
Salt ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste Mix it into the dry ingredients.
Milk and Butter 1 ¼ cups milk and 3 tablespoons melted butter Combine wet ingredients and add to dry mix.
Egg 1 large egg Beat and mix into the batter.
Pour batter onto a hot griddle, cook until golden brown on each side.
