Ingredients/Quantity Instructions
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat a 7x11-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
¼ teaspoon salt Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness.
⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper Sprinkle each piece of chicken on both sides with salt and pepper. Place 1 Swiss cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of each breast.
6 slices Swiss cheese Roll up each breast and secure with toothpicks. Place in the prepared baking dish and sprinkle chicken evenly with bread crumbs.
4 slices cooked ham Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink, 30 to 35 minutes. Remove from the oven, and place 1/2 Swiss cheese slice on top of each breast.
½ cup seasoned bread crumbs Return to the oven until cheese has melted, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove toothpicks and serve immediately.
