About Pastee Inc.

Pastee was founded by 6 dreamers who wanted to change the world. Pastee Inc. was founded in December 2023, when these dreamers finally decided to follow their dream to change the world.

It originally started as a pastry business, but later on, they also entered the industry of clothing and apparel, shoes, car care (car wax, car shampoo, etc.), and sports accessories.
